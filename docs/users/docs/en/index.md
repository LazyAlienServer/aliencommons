# AlienCommons User Guide

AlienCommons is a Minecraft-focused community for publishing, discussion, discovery, and community activity.

## Community Scope

Posts, articles, comments, profiles, and other community content should generally relate to Minecraft. Occasional nearby or casual topics are acceptable, such as hardware, software, creative tools, or everyday conversation, as long as they do not disrupt the community.

AlienCommons is strict about commercial noise. Advertising, spam, scams, promotional flooding, and low-value traffic bait may be removed even if they appear to be Minecraft-adjacent.

## Not Allowed

The following content is not allowed:

- Political, ideological, or highly sensitive social dispute content.
- Pornographic, sexually explicit, or sexualized content.
- Violent, graphic, threatening, or dangerous content.
- Religious controversy or proselytizing that disrupts the community.
- Hate, discrimination, dehumanization, or attacks against protected or identity-based groups.
- Illegal activity, scams, doxxing, credential theft, or instructions that enable harm.
- Personal attacks, harassment, bullying, intimidation, or targeted abuse.
- Spam, irrelevant promotion, bot-like posting, and other behavior that damages the community.

## Report Categories

When reporting content or a user profile, choose the closest reason:

- Off-topic or low-quality content.
- Spam, promotion, or flooding.
- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Hate or discrimination.
- Sexual content.
- Violence, gore, or threats.
- Politics, ideology, or social controversy.
- Religious controversy.
- Illegal, scam, or dangerous activity.
- Other problems.

Reports help moderators understand what happened. A report may still be rejected if the content is a good-faith nearby topic or does not violate the community rules.
